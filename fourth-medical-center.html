Muratsan Medical Center

University Hospital Complex “Muratsan” (former Pediatric Hospital N4) has been a part of YSMU since 2003. The university clinic, which is the clinical base of YSMU departments, is a multi-profile medical institution. We offer a wide range of medical services for different ages. The hospital has 15 clinics, polyclinic, laboratory and diagnostic center. Our hospital complex is equipped with modern medical equipment and provides outpatient medical care for more than 20,000 patients and inpatient care for more than 10,000 patients per year. The priority for us is the healing of our patients, care about their health. Our experienced doctors work for you and your children 365 days a year.
